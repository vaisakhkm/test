WelcomeDestiny’s Home Health Care

Destiny’s Home Health Care Inc.Destiny’s Home Health Care is a healthcare services company which has provided home healthcare and personal care services throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex since 2004. We provide patient-centered, comprehensive, and competent home healthcare combined with excellence in quality, service, and efficiency. We are respectful, friendly, helpful, accessible, and supportive of our patients and their families.

demo text ... Destiny’s Home Health Care is based on the principle that every human being has an innate dignity and value. Our multidisciplinary team enables those who have experienced setbacks, as a result of illness, to receive professional medical care in the privacy and comfort of their homes.

Benefits of Home Health Care

  • Patients are more comfortable at home and tend to recover more rapidly in the security and familiar surroundings of family and friends.
  • Home Health Care is less expensive than hospital or long-term care facilities.
  • Expanded range of services can now be provided in a home setting, such as X-Rays and EKG’s due to advancement in technology.
